What is solution ?

We proposed the E-blood ecosystem . The first app of its kind through which you can easily arrange blood in no time.

This app contains information about people of all blood groups within your Contact List. You will be able to contact users through SMS or Call them when you are in need of blood.

How we gonna achieve?

Blood Awareness : Encourage people to check their blood group and make them donate at-least one time .

Co-ordination : Help existing infrastructure for smooth operation technically.

Data Collection and Maintenance : Store blood group information and corresponding phone number

Volunteer
